Welcome to saveNOrg! saveNOrg is an app that will help you store and manage your notes.

Making a note is as easy as:

  1. Click the Add button
  2. Named your note
  3. Start taking notes!

If you think we are missing a feature, open an issue in github: saveNOrg, will try to add it just for you!
